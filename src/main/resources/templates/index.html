Blaff

Welcome to Blaff! The imposter game where you get to manipulate your friends and show them who the real blaffer is!

▶ Click the arrow for further instructions

The goal of the game is to prove ur loyalty to your friends. You will get assigned either civilian or the imposter. The goal is to blend in and answer questions or say relevant words related to the assigned word to keep your friends from voting you out as the imposter. If that happens.. GAME OVER FOR YOU. Good luck!
